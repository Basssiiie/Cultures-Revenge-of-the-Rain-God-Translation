After a long march we finally reached Maradorn's hut. Before we would even descend the small, snow-covered mountain, we already met familiar faces. His sons greeted us.
\n
\n
"Welcome, my brothers. I'm sure you want to see our father. But unfortunately you're too late. Our father is dying and we do not know when Odin will bring him to Valhalla for the festive table.
\n
\n
Getting ready for a big ride? Soren will accompany you. He is brave and strong and will be of use to you on your journey."
\n
\n
It was very painful for us to accept the loss of Maradorn, who was one of our greatest heroes.  But we were equally happy for him, for soon Odin would receive him and he would wait for us at the warriors' table.
