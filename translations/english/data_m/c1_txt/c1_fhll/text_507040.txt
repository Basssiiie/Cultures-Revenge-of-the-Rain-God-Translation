When we reached the small Mayan city on the banks of the Silver Lake, we were met with blatant rejection.
\n
\n
We saw some women hiding their children and closing the shutters of their houses when we arrived. Finally, a group of men who appeared to be members of the city council came into our path.
\n
\n
"What do you want from us," cried their leader, "are you not from the tribe of the false Rain God? Have you not already brought enough disaster on us?"
\n
\n
Our scouts no longer had an answer to this and withdrew sadly. But now we were at least certain that my uncle was holed up in the impassable swampland to the north.
