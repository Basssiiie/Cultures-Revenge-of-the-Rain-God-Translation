Our scouts could see the extent of the destruction from afar. The village of the Red Wings had been badly battered.
Everywhere you saw the wounded in their destroyed tents. It pained me when the once-proud people begged us for help.
\n
\n
With a handshake, I gave the overjoyed tribal elder my word that I would help with the reconstruction. Our primary goal now was to help these poor people who so desperately needed our help.
\n
\n
But I swore by Odin: once we found a way to resume the trail of the unknown enemy, we would take responsibility!
